The Idea

Problem

Consultants waste months searching for exit opportunities through generic job boards that aren't built for them. Most send 200+ applications, hear back from a handful, and rarely find relevant roles. The process is time-consuming, inefficient, and broken.

Solution

NextStep is a talent intelligence platform that curates exit opportunities for consultants and matches them using AI. Candidates set their preferences once and receive a personalised shortlist of relevant roles to their inbox every week. Privacy is protected throughout.

Business model

NextStep is free for candidates. The platform generates revenue by charging employers for access to the curated talent pool of consultants actively exploring their next move.
