Creator Share Ownership Plan (CSOP)

Co-owners, not paid promoters.

Our CSOP (Creator Share Ownership Plan) gives creators real equity in NextStep. Instead of paying influencer fees, we make creators co-owners with skin in the game. They promote because they own it. Think PRIME or Feastables, but for SaaS.
