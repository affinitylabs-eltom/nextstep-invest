The Vision
Recruitment is a trillion-dollar industry that technology has barely touched. AI is about to change that.

Job boards digitized listings. LinkedIn digitized networking. But the core of hiring still runs on cold outreach, gut feel, and expensive middlemen. Despite decades of incremental tech, the process hasn't fundamentally changed.

AI changes the equation entirely. For the first time, intelligent systems can truly understand what professionals want and what companies need. This is the Uber moment for how talent meets opportunity.

NextStep exists to make that shift happen.

The Problem

A $1 trillion industry that still runs on spam and guesswork.

A senior consultant opens LinkedIn. 47 recruiter messages. None relevant. Meanwhile, a growth-stage startup has been searching for that exact profile for 6 months. Both sides lose.

Candidate Side
60-hour weeks, zero time for a real job search
Hundreds of applications. A handful of responses. None that fit.
Drowning in irrelevant alerts and recruiter spam
Employer Side
The talent they want doesn't show up on job boards
Flooded with unqualified applicants for every role
The best consultants are passive. They never apply.
The Gap
The best talent never applies. The best companies never find them.

The Product

Your entire career search. Fully autonomous.

A personal AI agent that runs your whole recruitment process. From finding roles to applying to preparing for interviews. You set it up once. It does the rest.

STEP 01
Tell it what you want
Chat with your AI agent like you'd message a recruiter. Share your CV, your ambitions, what matters to you. It learns everything in one conversation.
STEP 02
It hunts for you
Your agent scans thousands of roles, scores them against your profile, and sends you only the ones worth your time. Every week, automatically.
STEP 03
Apply, prep, land
From tailored applications to interview coaching. Your agent handles the process end-to-end. You focus on the work. It focuses on your future.
